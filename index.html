About Miracle Events

Led by founder and event manager Rikesh Karna, Miracle Events is a premier event management company based in the heart of Kathmandu. Our mission is to blend local expertise with international standards, ensuring every event is a seamless, memorable success.

We believe that a great event is a work of art. From meticulous planning to flawless execution, Rikesh and his passionate team are committed to bringing your unique vision to life, whether it's a high-profile corporate function, a vibrant marketing campaign, or the wedding of your dreams.
